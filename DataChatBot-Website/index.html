Information is everywhere. But finding the right data, right when you need it? That's the maze.

Even with existing documentation, critical insights get buried. Teams battle to pinpoint information, leading to frustrating delays, duplicated efforts, and critical missed opportunities.

The real bottleneck isn't the lack of data—it's the struggle to access it. It's time to make your knowledge instantly discoverable.

How It Works: The Magic of RAG + LLM

The tool combines RAG and LLM to answer questions by retrieving relevant info from your local knowledge base and generating accurate, context-aware responses.

Visualizing the RAG Process

1. Building Our Knowledge Core

We start by gathering and processing relevant information from diverse sources like Alation, SQL query history, wikis, Google Docs, and Slack, creating a comprehensive knowledge base.

2. Understanding Your Query

When you ask a question, an advanced Large Language Model (LLM) first works to accurately understand the intent and nuances of your request.

3. Pinpointing Relevant Data (RAG)

Our Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) system then efficiently searches and retrieves the most pertinent pieces of information from the established knowledge base.

4. Generating Your Answer

Finally, the LLM synthesizes its understanding of your question with the retrieved data to generate a clear, concise, and context-aware answer.
